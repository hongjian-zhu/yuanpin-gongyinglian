FAQ / 常见问题

Answers buyers usually need before sending a serious inquiry.

Can I request MOQ and wholesale price? / 可以询问 MOQ 和批发价吗？

Yes. Please send interested products, country, buyer type, and estimated quantity. 可以，请发送产品、国家、买家类型和预计数量。

Do you support OEM/ODM? / 支持贴牌定制吗？

Logo, label, color, packaging, display box, and bundle requests can be discussed by product category. 可按品类沟通 Logo、标签、颜色、包装、展示盒和套装需求。

Can I get samples? / 可以拿样品吗？

Sample availability, cost, and lead time should be confirmed after the product and quantity are clear. 样品是否可做、费用和时间需要按具体产品确认。

How long is delivery time? / 交期多久？

Delivery time depends on product category, quantity, packaging, and customization. 交期取决于品类、数量、包装和是否定制。

Can I request a catalog or price list? / 可以索取目录或价格表吗？

Yes. Serious buyers can request product catalog, SKU list, MOQ, packaging details, and quotation files. 可以，真实批发买家可索取目录、SKU 清单、MOQ、包装和报价资料。
